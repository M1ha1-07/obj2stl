Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Everything happens locally

This tool is 100% client-side. We don't have a backend server for file processing, so your 3D models are never uploaded anywhere. The conversion from OBJ to STL happens directly in your browser's memory using JavaScript.

2. No tracking

There are no tracking scripts, analytics tools, or ads on this site. I don't know who is using the site or what models are being converted.

3. Cookies

We don't use any cookies. If we add custom settings in the future, they will be saved to your browser's localStorage, which never leaves your device.

Verify it yourself

You can check the network tab in your browser's dev tools—no requests are made when you convert a file. You can even disconnect from Wi-Fi right before clicking the file, and it will still work perfectly.
